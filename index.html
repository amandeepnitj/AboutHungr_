Who we are
Hungr is a technology company that connects people with the best of their neighborhoods across all provinces of Canada. We enable local businesses to meet consumers’ needs of ease and convenience, and, in turn, generate new ways for people to earn, work, and live. By building the last-mile logistics infrastructure for local commerce, we’re fulfilling our mission to grow and empower local economies.
Who we serve

Customers

With thousands of restaurants, convenience stores, pet stores, grocery stores, and more at your fingertips, Hungr delivers the best of your neighborhood on-demand.

Merchants

Reach new customers, market your store, and grow your business by offering delivery, pickup, and direct online ordering with Hungr.

Delivery Guys

Delivering with Hungr means earning money when and how you want. Dash long term or for a goal, and do it all on your own terms.

Empowering local economies

Empowering local economies means ensuring that people have equal access to opportunity to reach to their full potential. Learn how to create positive impact for our communities, from business owners to delivery persons, to our non profit partners.
